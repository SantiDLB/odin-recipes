Buttermilk Pancakes

This is how the pancakes will look like after following the recipe

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Leaveners
  • Salt
  • Buttermilk
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Combine flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt in a large bowl.
  2. Beat the buttermilk, milk, eggs, and melted butter together in a separate
    bowl. Do not combine the wet and dry ingredients until right before you
    cook the pancakes.
  3. For the fluffiest results, you shouldnt mix the dry ingrdients with the wet
    ingredients until right before you plan to cook.
