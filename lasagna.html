Pesto Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 pack of lasagna noodles
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 package frozen chopped spinach, thawed
  • 7 ounces of basil pesto
  • 30 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • half a teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 9 ounces Alfredo-style pasta sauce
  • 1/4 grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add lasagna and cook for 8 to 10 minutesor until al dente; drain.
  3. In large skillet over medum heat, saute onion in olive oil until tender. Stir in spinach; remove from heat and stir in pesto
  4. In a large bowl mix ricotta cheese, egg, salt, pepper and nutmeg

  5. In a 3-quart greased baking dish, layer the noodles then spinach mixture,

    followed by ricotta mixture. Sprinkle with mozzarella cheese. Repeat the

    layers ending with noodles on top. Spread Alfredo sauce on top of noodles

    and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
